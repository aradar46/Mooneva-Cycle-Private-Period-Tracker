Mooneva is a private, fully offline period and cycle tracker. All data stays on your device — no account, no cloud sync, no ads.

<b>Features</b>
<ul>
<li>Period and cycle tracking with predictions</li>
<li>Fertile window and ovulation estimates</li>
<li>Daily log: mood, symptoms, flow intensity, discharge</li>
<li>PMS window warnings</li>
<li>Trends and cycle history</li>
<li>Clinical report export</li>
<li>Discrete mode (disguises app icon)</li>
<li>PIN lock with configurable timeout</li>
<li>Reminder notifications</li>
<li>Birth control mode (hides fertile window)</li>
<li>Fully offline — no internet permission required</li>
</ul>

<b>Privacy</b>
No data ever leaves your device. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services.
